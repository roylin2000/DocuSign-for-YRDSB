Liability Insurance
Students and community sponsors are only covered by the Board’s insurance plan for liability losses arising from the students’ defined activities within the 40 hours of
community involvement or other limit set by the high school if the Notification and Completion of Planned Community Involvement Activities form is completed.
Community sponsors will be responsible for ensuring that their liability insurance will protect them for their involvement in this program.
